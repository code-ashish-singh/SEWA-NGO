Empowering Families, Building a Stronger Society

SEWA (Self Employed Women’s Association) is a social organization dedicated to empowering women, men, and children to create a better and more sustainable future. Our mission is to promote self-reliance, equal opportunities, and dignified livelihoods through education, skill development, healthcare, and employment support. By strengthening families at the grassroots level, we aim to build a more inclusive and empowered society.

Skill Development & Training

We provide vocational training and skill-building programs to help women and youth become self-reliant. These programs are designed to create sustainable livelihood opportunities and financial independence.

Education & Child Support

Our education services focus on providing quality learning, school support, and a safe environment for children, ensuring a strong foundation for their future growth and success.

Healthcare & Wellness

We offer basic healthcare support, health awareness programs, and wellness initiatives for families, especially in underserved communities, to promote healthy and dignified living.

Employment & Livelihood Support

We assist individuals and self-employed workers by connecting them with job opportunities, resources, and financial guidance to improve income stability and long-term security.
